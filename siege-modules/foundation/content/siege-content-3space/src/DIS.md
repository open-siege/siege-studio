### DIS File Format

Represents the high level information for an interior shape.

Forms part of a trio of formats which describe interior shapes, also including:
* [DIG](DIG.md)
* [DIL](DIL.md)

### See Also

DIS files are typically embedded into [VOL files](VOL).
